About Us

WegaHaat Drug Agency

Your Trusted Partner in Pharmaceutical Distribution

Established in 2009, WegaHaat Drug Agency has emerged as a leading pharmaceutical distribution company in India, serving as the critical link between pharmaceutical manufacturers and healthcare providers. With over 15 years of experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, authenticity, and excellence in pharmaceutical supply chain management.

Our state-of-the-art warehousing facilities, equipped with temperature-controlled storage and advanced inventory management systems, ensure that every product maintains its integrity from manufacturer to end-user. We strictly adhere to Good Distribution Practices (GDP) guidelines and maintain comprehensive documentation for complete traceability.

Our Mission

To ensure uninterrupted access to genuine, high-quality medicines for every healthcare institution in India through efficient distribution networks, maintaining the highest standards of integrity, compliance, and customer service.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted pharmaceutical distribution partner, recognized for operational excellence, technological innovation, and unwavering commitment to patient safety through reliable medicine supply.
